Glossary

Electric cellA source of electrical energy with a positive (+) and negative (−) terminal.
BulbA device that glows when current passes through its filament.
Electric circuitA complete closed path through which current can flow.
Open circuitAn incomplete path. Current cannot flow, so the bulb does not glow.
Closed circuitA complete path. Current can flow, so the bulb glows.
TerminalThe two ends of a cell or bulb where wires are connected.
